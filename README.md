                                                                 OverView
This Budget Management Program helps users track their income, expenses, and monthly budgets,It allows you to organize you finances, monitor spending, and generate financial summaries

                                                         HOW TO USE THE PROGRAM
When the program starts, type one of the available commands shown in the menu. You can also type: Help, To see all available commands at given time

                                                        FEATURES AND WHAT THEY DO
add income:
          Lets you reocrd your source of income (ex: Job, Allowance, etc..)
          The amount entered is then added to your total balance.
          This helps track how much money you are earning.

add expense:
          Lets you record money in a specific category.
          The expsense must belong to a budget categoty that has already been made.
          The amount is then subtracted from your balance.
          The program shows you how much of you budget you have left and lets you know if you are close to or over the budget.

set budget:
          Allows you to create or edit a spending category (example: Food, Transportation, Entertainment).
          You assign a maximum spending amount for that category.
          This helps control your spending and stay organized.

view transac:
            Shows all recorded transactions.
            Shows whether each entry was income or expense, the date and time, description, and the amount.
            Useful for reviewing your spending history.

view summary:
            Shows a full financial summary including:
                Total income
                Total expenses
                Remaining balance
                Budget usage by category
                And if you are over or under total budget
              Helps Understand your over all financial situation.

monthly report:
              Generate a detailed financial report for the month.
              Includes:
              Total income and expenses
              Net income
              saving rate
              Top 3 spending categories
              Budget utilization percentage
              Categorie that are close to exceeding their budget
            This helps analyze spending patterns and improve financial planning.
save data:
        Saves all income, expenses, budgets, and transactions to a file.
        Use this to make sure your financial data is not lost.

clear data:
          Deletes all stored income, expenses, budgets, and transaction history.
          This CANNOT be undone.

exit:
    Saves your data automatically.


                                                         PURPOSE OF THE PROGRAM
  Manage money responsibly
  Stay within spending limits
  Track financial habits
  Improve budgeting skills
